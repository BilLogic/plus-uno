The **Request tutor** off-canvas is triggered from the in-session Session Controls overflow menu.
A **lead** posts a structured request (number of tutors, closing time, reason) then confirms the Slack
message; a **regular tutor** requests a lead tutor with a one-tap Slack message. Both end in a **sent**
confirmation state.
