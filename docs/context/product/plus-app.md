# PLUS App — Product Landscape

## Mission

AI is rewriting our economy, but global K-12 math proficiency is at a historic low due to lack of content mastery and emotional support. PLUS makes expert one-on-one tutoring effective and scalable ($20/student/month vs. $2,000 traditional) by combining AI superpowers (pattern tracking, real-time insights) with human superpowers (trust, judgment, reading the room). As the system operates it improves — AI gets sharper, training becomes faster, cost per student drops.

## Service System

```
Foundations → fund PLUS → trains/pays college STEM tutors
    ↓
Tutors → deliver 1:1 instruction → K-12 students
    ↓
Schools → share progress data → Researchers → prove impact
    ↓
Evidence → feeds back to foundations (completing the loop)
```

500+ college tutors across CMU, Pitt, and Duquesne serve 3,000+ marginalized K-12 students via Zoom and Pencil Spaces.

## AI + Human Loop

| Phase | AI Role | Human Role |
|-------|---------|------------|
| Before session | Coach — simulations & scenario training | Tutor practices pedagogical skills |
| During session | Co-Pilot — surfaces momentum, struggles, talking points | Tutor decides how to respond |
| After session | Reflection guide — prompts structured review | Tutor captures insights |
| Between sessions | Personal coach — weekly growth reports | Tutor refines strategy |

## Integrations

| Platform | Role |
|----------|------|
| **Pencil Spaces** | Primary tutoring — rich interaction metadata |
| **Zoom** | Secondary sessions — breakout rooms |
| **Slack** | Escalations, supervisor alerts, call-offs |
| **Intercom** | Help Center, tooltip onboarding |
| **Figma** | Design source of truth, component library |
| **Acuity** | Session scheduling (embedded iframe) |
| **Mathia / IXL** | Math content platforms |
| **Workday / Handshake** | Tutor recruitment |

## Stakeholder Views

- **Tutors** — in-session tools, reflections, training, growth tracking
- **Administrators** — operational health, tutor performance tables
- **Researchers** — AI-consolidated insights from millions of interactions
