The surface container is the **Main-region shell** — the white content area every page is built on. Baked in: `--color-surface` background, Surface-tier padding (`--size-surface-pad-x/y`), gap, radius, and no elevation. It **fills the width it is given**: the page/shell owns the width and the breakpoint mode, the container just adapts. Children are the content slot. It mirrors the published `Pattern/Surface container` Figma component (auto mode + fill), which also carries the adaptive 12-column content grid.
